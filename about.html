About Us

ALTOM Building Services is a Phoenix-based general contracting company specialising in residential remodeling. We work with a trusted network of skilled tradespeople: plumbers, electricians, tilers, carpenters, and finishing specialists, to deliver complete projects under one roof.

Every project is contractor-led from start to finish. That means clear timelines, honest pricing, and a single point of contact throughout. No surprises, no subcontractor runaround.

We don't just manage projects. We build relationships. Many of our clients come back to us for their next renovation, and most of our new work comes through personal referrals. That track record is something we're proud of and something we work hard to maintain on every job.

From a single bathroom refresh to a full whole-home transformation, we bring the same level of care and attention to every project regardless of scale.
