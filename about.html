Meet Tom
Tom's photo

Tom Jarosiewicz is the founder and lead contractor of ALTOM Building Services, bringing years of hands-on experience in residential remodeling and renovation to every project he takes on.

From full kitchen and bathroom renovations to whole-home remodels, Tom approaches each job with the same commitment to quality craftsmanship and clear communication. He works closely with homeowners from the first consultation through to the final walkthrough, ensuring the finished result matches the vision.

Based in the Phoenix area, Tom has built a reputation for reliable, high-quality work and straightforward pricing. Whether you have a clear plan in mind or just an idea of what you want, he'll work with you to make it happen.
